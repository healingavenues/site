    •   Terry MacIntosh N. D., licensed doctor of naturopathic medicine has been practicing individualized wholistic healthcare for more than 25 years. He has served as secretary of the Ontario Naturopathic Association, a member of I.N.E.R. (Institute of Naturopathic Education and Research) was a supervising naturopathic doctor at the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine, and has authored numerous professional and newspaper articles on natural health care.
      Students at the C.C.N.M. are required to complete a university degree in conventional health sciences followed by a rigorous, full-time 4 year program at the naturopathic college. Graduates must then wrlte board and provincial exams in order to practice in Canada. Upon successful completion graduates are awarded the title N.D. - Doctor of Naturopathy.
      As one of the founding practitioners at the Guelph Community Health Centre, Terry shared a vision that healthcare of the future will combine the expertise of various healthcare providers working in cooperation with one another for the benefit of the patient. Amongst the healthcare practitioners at this clinic were two naturopathic doctors, three medical doctors, two nurse practitioners, a chiropractor, two massage therapists, social workers, and administrative staff. This clinic was truly a groundbreaking effort and a model of a future system that will save both individuals and government time and money. Doctor MacIntosh also provides specific health-based and motivational talks to organizations and businesses to help keep members energetic, informed, healthy, and by extension increase productivity and reduce absenteeism.
